Configurable/Modular Industrial & Medical PSUs

Advanced Energy produces a wide range of medium to high power AC-DC power supplies, primarily composed of highly configurable modular power supplies and rack-mounted bulk power units.  These include individual power modules with output rantings of 36W up to 12,000W, facilitating customers to configure bulk power systems with an output capability of up to 30 kW.  Many of these products are available in medically approved versions and feature comprehensive built-in intelligence.  They are recognized for their exceptional performance, reliability, and cost-effectiveness.
